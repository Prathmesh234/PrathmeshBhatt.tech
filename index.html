About me

Hi , I am Prathmesh Bhatt , a Computer science undergrad at University of Texas at Dallas. I have programming knowledge of 3+ years and have worked on C++ , C# for Arduino , JAVA , Swift , React, node js etc programming languages along with coreML , AR Kit , coreUI and Machine learning in IOS Development . Moreover I have experience with databases such as Mongodb , Firebase and Postgresql.

I am involved in a lot of campus activities such as soccer and several tech clubs. I am the Head Event Coordinator for HacktoHelp which is one of the biggest Hackathon organizations on campus . Along with that , I am heavily involved in Google Student Developers club as well as Association of Computing and Machinery at UTD . Furthermore , I am working under Dr Ravi Prakash as a research assistant . In addition , I am an active Brother in the Delta Epsilon Psi Fraternity which is the biggest South Asian Fraternity on campus .

My hobbies include playing competitive soccer , being active in charity as well as fundraising events for my Fraternity which is one of the largest donors to JDRF - Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation. For more information , please visit - https://deltaepsilonpsi.org/national/jdrf. Moreover , I have a keen interest in photography as well as Weightlifiting .
